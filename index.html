About Me

At the crossroads of technology and youthful zest, I find myself—an 18-year-old Computer Science devotee hailing from Nepal. This vibrant journey encompasses a symphony of tech, gaming, and wanderlust. Technology is my North Star. With nimble fingers dancing on the keyboard, I compose symphonies of code, crafting digital marvels from scratch. The euphoria of debugging an intricate algorithm mirrors the strategic puzzles I conquer in the virtual realms of video games. A seamless fusion of logic and imagination, these passions feed into each other, nurturing a holistic understanding of innovation. Beyond the binary code, gaming is my refuge. Virtual worlds unfurl before me, offering solace and adventure. As I navigate through diverse landscapes, I discover narratives that resonate, strengthening my creative sinews. These pixels aren't just a pastime; they are portals to profound stories and experiences. The allure of technology and gaming is balanced by a craving for real-world exploration. From Nepal's serene valleys to its lofty Himalayan peaks, every vista whispers tales of culture and diversity. These expeditions don't just satiate my wanderlust; they instill empathy and a global perspective. In this whirlwind, Computer Science, gaming, and travel converge, shaping an individual who embraces innovation, seeks the extraordinary, and believes in the power of interconnected passions. As I stand on the brink of adulthood, I am poised to amplify my passions and embark on a lifelong odyssey—unearthing the mysteries of codes, conquering virtual realms, and unifying cultures in a tapestry woven with the threads of technology, gaming, and exploration.
